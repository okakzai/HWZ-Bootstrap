Hiltz & Wantuch LLC is a specialized law firm that focuses its practice on bankruptcy related matters. John Hiltz and Kathy Wantuch formed Hiltz & Wantuch LLC in early 2010 to use their unique set of skills and experience to better serve their clients. John and Kathy began their careers within the Bankruptcy Court system here in Chicago. They both worked directly under a Judge as law clerks in the Bankruptcy Court for the Northern District of Illinois. Since then they have focused their legal careers in the field of bankruptcy law. This experience enables Hiltz & Wantuch LLC to successfully navigate the Bankruptcy Court and provide effective legal solutions to its clients.

Whether you are an individual contemplating bankruptcy, a small business facing possible liquidation or reorganization, or a creditor looking to retain a lawyer to advocate for your rights, choose Hiltz & Wantuch LLC.

Hiltz & Wantuch LLC prides itself on exceptional customer service and zealous advocacy. We offer free initial consultations and offer individualized fee arrangements at competitive prices.
